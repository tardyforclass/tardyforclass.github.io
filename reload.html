How to use

  1. Allow the app to know your location
  2. Exit the app and wait for people to click on your marker and contact you with the phone number you signed up with or you can click on another user's marker and contact them by clicking the number on their marker
  3. If you change locations, simply open the app and click the load button and you marker will be updated with your new location

How To Pay

  1. We recommend that you pay and get paid with card due to it being more secure.
  2. When paying or getting paid with card, always send the money first to whoever is delivering your item. If they decide to scam you and not deliver, you can always cancel the charge and they won't get a dime. You are free to pay and get paid with cash but please note that there is a higher risk of fraud
  3. It's recommended that you agree on a transaction amount before you deliver any goods or before you accept any delivered goods
